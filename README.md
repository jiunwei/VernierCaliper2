#	Vernier Caliper Simulator 2
 
VernierCaliper2 is a simple iOS app for learners to practice reading a vernier caliper. It features two scales of different precision and a timed game mode with GameKit leaderboards.
 
## 	Usage
 
Slide your finger to move the blue vernier scale horizontally or the red object vertically. Enter your answer and press â€œCheck Answer" to see if you got the answer right. Settings may be changed from the toolbar at the bottom of the screen.
